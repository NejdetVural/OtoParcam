# 1. Introduction

## 1.1 Purpose

OtoParcam is a web-based inventory and sales management system designed for businesses that sell used auto spare parts.

The system enables customers to quickly find compatible spare parts based on vehicle information, while allowing administrators to 
efficiently manage products, inventory, customers, and orders through a centralized dashboard.

## 1.2 Problem Statement
Many used auto spare part businesses still rely on phone calls, WhatsApp conversations, spreadsheets, or manual inventory tracking.

These traditional methods create several problems:

- Difficult product searching
- Inaccurate inventory management
- Wrong part selection
- Slow customer response time
- Limited reporting capabilities

OtoParcam aims to solve these issues by providing a centralized digital platform.

# 3. Target Audience

The system is designed for two primary user roles.

## Administrator

The administrator has full access to the system and is responsible for managing all business operations.

Main responsibilities include:

- Managing products
- Managing categories
- Managing vehicle brands and models
- Managing customer accounts
- Managing orders
- Updating inventory
- Viewing reports and dashboard statistics

## Customer

Customers can use the platform to search and browse available spare parts based on their vehicle information.

Main capabilities include:

- Register and log in
- Search products
- Filter products by brand, model and category
- View product details
- Add products to favorites
- Place orders
- Track order status
- Manage profile information
- Customers can browse products without creating an account. However, registration is required to submit a purchase request,
track request status, and manage favorites.
